About Our Club


Vivekananda Club, located in Maynaguri, Jalpaiguri, is a social, cultural and sports organization dedicated to community development, youth empowerment and social service. Inspired by Swami Vivekananda's ideals, the club organizes welfare programs, festivals, sports and community activities.

Our Activities


⚽ Sports Events

Organizing football, cricket, volleyball and local tournaments.

🎭 Cultural Programs

Celebrating festivals, cultural events, and youth competitions.

❤️ Social Welfare

Blood donation camps, environmental awareness, and community service.
