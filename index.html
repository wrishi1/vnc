About Our Club


Vivekananda Club, located in Maynaguri, Jalpaiguri, is a social, cultural, and sports organization dedicated to community development, youth empowerment, and social service activities. Inspired by the ideals of Swami Vivekananda, the club actively participates in welfare programs, festivals, and sports events.

Our Activities


Sports Events

Organizing football, cricket, volleyball and local tournaments.

Cultural Programs

Celebrating festivals, cultural events, and youth competitions.

Social Welfare

Blood donation camps, environmental awareness, and community service.
